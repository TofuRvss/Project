Ruins and Monuments

The ruins of Altun Ha (left) and Xunantunich(right) are some of the many examples of Mayan settlement within the young nation. Altun Ha is one of the furhtest excavated ruins in the nation, with in the monument lies a jade carving of the Mayan sun god. Xunantunich, Mayan for "The Stone Maiden" holds the second largest building standing in the nation. The highest point being on El Castillio "The Castle" standing at 130 feet from top to bottom.
